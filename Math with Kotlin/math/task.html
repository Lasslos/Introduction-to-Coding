Division? Pay attention if it rounds, and if yes up or down? What happens if you replace something like 3 / 2 by 3.0 / 2.0

Use / to do division. Kotlin has two major types of numbers. These are Integer and Double.. An Integer can represent every whole number, while a Double also supports decimal places. When doing an Integer Division, Kotlin will always round down.
